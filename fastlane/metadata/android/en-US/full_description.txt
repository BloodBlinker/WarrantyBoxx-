WarrantyBoxx is the only dedicated, fully offline, open-source warranty
tracker on F-Droid. It turns the moment of purchase into a 60-second entry that
guarantees you will never miss a warranty claim again.

Consumers lose money every year by failing to claim repairs and replacements on
products still under warranty. The problem is not awareness — it is friction:
receipts get lost, expiry dates are forgotten, and there is no single place to
track what is covered and for how long. WarrantyBoxx fixes that, without
asking you to create an account or share any data.

== Privacy by design ==

* No accounts, no cloud, no sync — everything stays on your device.
* Zero network calls. The app simply does not talk to the internet.
* No analytics, no crash-reporting SDKs, no third-party trackers.
* No Google Mobile Services or Firebase.

== Features ==

* Add items in seconds: name, purchase date and warranty duration are all you
  need. Expiry is calculated automatically.
* Optional details: brand, category, retailer, price, serial and model numbers,
  notes, and up to five photos of receipts or products.
* Dashboard: a warranty health ring, tappable summary chips, and your items
  sorted by expiry date with clear green/amber/red/grey status colours.
* Local reminders: get notified 30, 15, 7 and 1 day before a warranty expires
  (fully customisable per item). Reminders work entirely offline via Android's
  WorkManager — no Google services required.
* Search, filter and sort by name, brand, retailer, serial number, category,
  status, price or date.
* Claim Assistant: when a warranty is about to expire, a guided checklist helps
  you gather receipts, serial numbers and manufacturer contacts so your claim
  succeeds.
* Lifetime asset tracker: see the total value of everything you have under
  active warranty.
* Item templates for common products (laptop, TV, washing machine, and more)
  pre-fill typical warranty periods.
* Export your whole vault to CSV or a formatted PDF, or share a single item as
  plain text for a claim email — all through the standard Android share sheet.
* Home screen widget showing what is expiring soon.
* Archive of claimed items, with the resolution recorded — a tangible record of
  the money you have reclaimed.

== Accessibility ==

Status is always shown with both colour and an icon or text. Photos support
alt text, touch targets meet accessibility minimums, and the interface scales
with your system font size.

== Free forever ==

WarrantyBoxx is free and open source under the GPL-3.0. There are no ads, no
in-app purchases, and no premium tier. Ever.

Every warranty. Always remembered.
