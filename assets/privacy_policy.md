# WarrantyBoxx — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-06-10**

WarrantyBoxx is designed to respect your privacy completely.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** WarrantyBoxx collects no personal data of any kind.

## Where your data lives

All data you enter — item details, photos, notes, prices — is stored **only on
your device**, inside the app's private storage. It never leaves your phone
unless *you* explicitly choose to export or share it.

## Network access

WarrantyBoxx makes **zero network calls**. The app has no servers, no backend,
and no cloud sync. It works entirely offline.

## Third-party services

WarrantyBoxx contains:

- **No** Google Mobile Services or Firebase.
- **No** analytics or telemetry SDKs.
- **No** crash reporting SDKs.
- **No** advertising SDKs.
- **No** third-party trackers of any kind.

## Permissions

The app requests permissions only when you take an action that needs them:

- **Camera** — only when you tap "Take photo".
- **Photo/media access** — only when you tap "Choose from gallery".
- **Notifications** — only to deliver the warranty reminders you ask for.

You can use the app without granting any of these.

## Your exports

When you export data (CSV or PDF) or share an item, that data is handed to the
Android share sheet so you can choose where it goes (email, files, etc.). What
happens to it after that is governed by the app you share it with, not by
WarrantyBoxx.

## Contact

WarrantyBoxx is free and open source (GPL-3.0). Source code and issues:
see the project repository.
